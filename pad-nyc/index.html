What it is

A removable accessory drive for active manual wheelchair users. Custom motors and a 300 Wh battery pack carry you 15 to 20 miles per charge. The whole thing detaches in seconds, so the chair goes back to being light and transportable.

How it works

Snap on for the ride to work. Pull off to charge. The drive integrates with the chair's existing geometry without permanent modification, and the controls stay within thumb's reach.
