# Experimental Pump Design

Designed a fully parametric centrifugal pump:

Variables for:
- vane number
- vane pitch
- vane thickness
- impeller diameter
- impeller thickness
- open or closed impeller
- impeller thickness
- Inlet diameter
- Outlet diameter
- tolerances and more misc

Pump regenerated succesfully within a 2" - 20" diameter range with no issues and no rebuild issues related to other variables.  

Pump worked very well
