# Doppler Cooling Simulation

An interactive 1D simulation of laser cooling — a real lab technique that uses counter-propagating lasers and the Doppler effect to slow particles toward absolute zero. Built alongside Hamiltonian solver work during my time as a research assistant at Cooper Union.
