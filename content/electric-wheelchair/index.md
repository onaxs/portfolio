# Electric Wheelchair Attachment (PAD NYC)

Our team designed a compact accessory drive that safely assists our client (Victor Calise), an active manual wheelchair user, to reduce his pain, exhaustion, and injury risk from manual propulsion over long distances. Custom motors and batteries help our user tackle NYC's steepest hills while providing 15 to 20 miles of range. After use the device can be quickly detached to use the chair normally.

<img src="content/electric-wheelchair/images/PAD_Final_Poster.png" alt="Poster" width="1000">

## Testing and Specs

Specs at a glance:

- **Range:** 15–20 miles per charge
- **Top assist speed:** Limited to 7mph (capable of over 15mph)
- **Hill grade tested:** 3.5 deg (will test 10 deg soon)
- **Pack capacity:** 300wh 
- **Total system weight:** 15 lb

<img src="content/electric-wheelchair/images/hill_climb.gif" alt="Hill Climb" width="400">
Carried **375lb system weight up a 3.5 degree ramp** with power to spare (less than 4.5 degree ADA requirement)
